## CLIENT INTAKE PROCESS

### Version A — New Business (0–3 months)
- **Focus:** Build from scratch, tell the story, get first clients
- **Key questions:** Why did you start? What do you sell? How much? How to contact? What makes you different?
- **Mindset:** Client is scared and excited. Give them structure and clarity.

### Version B — Established Business (3+ months)
- **Focus:** Diagnose what's failing, scale what works, open new revenue streams
- **Key questions:** What has worked? What hasn't? What opportunities are missing? How much engagement?
- **Mindset:** Client already tried and is frustrated. Show them the problem is the strategy, not the business.

## MARKETING PSYCHOLOGY I APPLY

| Principle | How I Use It |
|---|---|
| Scarcity | "Solo me quedan 3 piezas esta noche" — activates purchase from FOMO |
| Social Proof | Real customer photos, families served numbers, reviews |
| Reciprocity | Free engraving, free shipping, free FAQ — people return the favor by buying |
| Price Anchoring | Show most expensive product first — everything else looks cheap |
| Authority | "First in Laredo to do this" — history and process build credibility |
| Urgency | "This offer is tonight only" — without urgency people say "later" and never return |
| Identity | The product isn't just an object — it's part of the Texan and family identity |
